üìñKaarak Kya Hai? (What is Kaarak?)

Kaarak ka matlab grammatical case hota hai. Ye show karta hai ki noun ya pronoun ka rishta sentence ke doosre shabd se kya hai. Hindi mein 8 kaarak hote hain jo vibhakti chinh (case markers) se pehchane jaate hain.

Jaise ki jab hum bolte hain "Ram ne khana khaya" - isme different words ka alag alag role hota hai. Kaarak hume ye samajhne mein madad karta hai ki sentence mein kon kya kar raha hai, kahan kar raha hai, kaise kar raha hai, etc.

üåü Simple Example:
"Ram kitaab padh raha hai"
Isme 'Ram' subject hai (Karta Karak), 'kitaab' object hai (Karma Karak).
